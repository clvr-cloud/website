What do we do?

With teams distributed around New Zealand and Argentina, we bring our expertise on building, running and operating highly available systems to small and medium enterprises enabling Digital Transformation.
Our expertise is based on years working for IT, aerospacial, banking and insurance companies.
We focus on:

Site Reliability Engineering

Run your applications on a highly available, fault tolerant platform, bringing resilience and stability to your customers while shipping new features and applications.
This involves Infrastructure as Code and Operation Support.

Agile & DevOps

Disrupt the business by deploying sofware quickly, accelerating innovation and reducing IT costs.
DevSecOps allows us to move ideas from development to production fast, securing and protecting your applications and environments via network and security polices.

Multi Cloud Strategies

By implementing a Cloud Native strategy, you ensure that the effort that you put into building, deploying and operating your applications is portable across cloud providers and platforms.
This stategy brings along Freedom of Choice,avoids vendor lock-in and exploits the benefits of cloud computing, such as elasticity

Coaching & Training

We actively giving workshops and training regarding SRE, Devops, cloud, docker and kubernetes.

Operation Support

We provide operations support and maintance of your environments and applications. Our stack is based on open-source technologies, such as Prometheus and Alert manager.

Innovation Labs

We are continously improving ourselves and learning new technologies and tools via our innovation labs. This is our key for continuous improvement.
